# Project Concept: Novias del Gato

## Introduction & Problem Statement
Since 1996, Hernando Tejada's "El Gato del Río" (The River Cat) has been a symbolic landmark in Cali, Colombia. In 2006, the project expanded with the arrival of the first fifteen "Novias del Gato" (The Cat's Brides) – a series of cat sculptures designed to accompany the bronze feline. By early 2026, this collection has grown into an "urban feline empire" of 38 sculptures distributed across the city.

However, this expansion has created a technical challenge: **data fragmentation and obsolescence.** Unlike traditional static monuments, these Cat Sculptures are dynamic; they are frequently relocated for maintenance or urban planning, and new pieces are added periodically. Currently, their exact locations often depend on informal or outdated sources, leading to a frustrating experience for tourists and citizens alike.

## The Proposal: A Geographic Oracle
This project proposes a digital platform designed to manage a real-time ecosystem of geographic and cultural data. The application bridges the gap between static art and dynamic urban life through:

1.  **API-First Architecture:** A decoupled backend ensuring data can be consumed by web, mobile, and smart-city services.
2.  **Collaborative Consensus Engine:** A validation system where sculpture locations are updated via citizen reports, processed through a trust algorithm.
3.  **Visual Check-ins:** Using geolocation and image recognition to "unlock" historical and multimedia content, encouraging physical exploration.
4.  **Collective Memory Repository:** A space for historical context, urban sounds, and curated narratives.

## User Stories (MVP)
*   **As an Explorer:** I want to see a map with validated locations of the cats so I can plan my route without finding empty pedestals.
*   **As a Contributor:** I want to report that a cat has been moved or is under maintenance by sending a photo and my location to keep the catalog updated.
*   **As a Collector:** I want to "capture" a cat using my mobile camera (Visual Check-in) to unlock its technical details and track my progress.
*   **As a Moderator:** I want to review disputed reports to confirm location changes when the consensus algorithm is inconclusive.
